Founded in 2010, South Cove Strategies is a government relations and public affairs firm based in Seattle, Washington.

With experience working on a variety of legislative issue areas, human services, higher education, health care, housing, basic education, budget and appropriations, delivering results for clients is the number one goal for South Cove Strategies.

Our clients range from local non-profits to regional trade associations, community coalitions to elected officials. We will work with you to tailor a legislative strategy that works best for your organization.

At South Cove Strategies, we take pride in helping you navigate the politics and policy in Olympia to advance your legislative priorities.
