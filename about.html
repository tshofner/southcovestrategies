Alex Hur is the lead lobbyist for South Cove Strategies. From 2004-2010, he worked on federal and legislative campaigns. In 2012, Alex managed the levy campaigns for Seattle Public Libraries and the Seattle Seawall.

Prior to founding South Cove Strategies, Alex was a legislative assistant for Speaker Frank Chopp. Alex has a B.A. in Political Science from the University of Washington.
